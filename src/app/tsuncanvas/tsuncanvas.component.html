Hayase Yuuka

Hayase Yuuka (早瀬ユウカ) is one of the students studying in Millennium Science School who wields a Sub-Machine Gun.

She is a member of the Seminar and the Treasurer of her school.

Keqing

Keqing (Chinese: 刻晴 Kèqíng) is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact.
As the Yuheng of the Liyue Qixing, she is someone who seeks her own answers instead of idly letting chaos run amok in Liyue. She chooses her own path with her own power and ability, instead of letting the gods determine her fate.
